LG K330 - Help.

1. Brief information

- Model: LG K330

- Flash: 8 GB Hynix eMMC

- OS: Android


2. EMMC pinout.

Please carefully solder all eMMC pins as shown on pinout picture.


3. Smart Repair.

1. Please choose your device on "Model Settings" tab.

2. Disconnect battery and USB cable.

3. Connect your device.

4. Press "Smart Repair" button.

5. Choose repair file which you want to use (if there are more than one).

6. Choose partitions which need to be repaired and press the Repair button.


Emergency Mode

After succesfull Repair of your device You have to put it to "Emergency Mode" to re-flash. To do this please perform the following steps:

1. Reconnect battery;

2. Press and hold "Vol+" key;

3. Connect USB Cable.
